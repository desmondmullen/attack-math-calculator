Star Wars RPG Attack Math Calculator

A tool for tweaking numbers for assignment 4 (jQuery)
at UNC Full Stack Bootcamp, December 2018


Tweak health and attack numbers, select options, then click Run Scenarios to see (at the bottom of the window) the outcome of every possible fighting scenario.

verbose output no damage on winning*

*in the demo video, the player receives no damage in a round where he/she defeats the enemy. There is no mention of this in the video or in the assignment's instructions (and it doesn't make sense), but if you watch very carefully you can see that this is what is happening. Thanks to Marcia Moss for spotting it.
